ArthaRoshni — Free CBSE Economics for Classes 10, 11 & 12

ArthaRoshni is India's free, offline-first learning platform for CBSE Economics. It covers the complete NCERT syllabus for Class 10, Class 11 and Class 12 with chapter-wise notes, hand-drawn infographics and diagrams, a 2,800+ question practice bank, 500 hands-on technical labs, and four free certification paths. No fees, no subscription, and every chapter works offline once loaded.

What you can study

  • Class 10 Economics — Development, Sectors of the Indian Economy, Money and Credit, Globalisation, and Consumer Rights, each with a "Bihar Lens" chapter grounding the theory in local data.
  • Class 11 — Statistics for Economics & Indian Economic Development — all 8 IED chapters written in full: the colonial drain of wealth, planning and the Green Revolution, the 1991 LPG reforms, human capital, rural development, employment, environment, and a India–China–Pakistan comparison.
  • Class 12 — Introductory Macroeconomics & Microeconomics — national income, money and banking, determination of income and employment, consumer behaviour, production and costs, and market forms.
  • India's Economic Blueprint — an interactive 8-chapter special tracing the Indian economy from 1947 to a $5-trillion trajectory, with a clickable India map.

Practice and labs

Over 2,800 authentic short-answer and multiple-choice questions across 29 chapters, with instant feedback, flashcard mode and saved scores. The 500-lab technical catalogue spans ten tracks — Python for economics, data visualisation, statistics, econometrics I and II, behavioural economics, NLP, climate economics, international and development economics, and capstone projects — all runnable free in Google Colab. Labs require a free account.

Free certification paths

Four structured paths — ACE-10, ACE-11, ACE-12 and PRO — take you from Class 10 foundations to research-ready data skills. Finish the chapters, clear the quizzes; the path is free from start to finish.
